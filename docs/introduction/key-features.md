### Phase Timers
Games are designed to create **intense pressure**:

#### Discussion Phase: **4 Minutes**

- Forces quick thinking and split-second decisions
- No time for perfect information gathering

- **Emotional Pressure**: Stress reveals authentic reactions
- **Skill Expression**: Better players thrive under pressure
- **Prevents Analysis Paralysis**: Forces action, not overthinking
