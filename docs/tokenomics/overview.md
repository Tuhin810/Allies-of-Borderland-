- **Circulating Supply**: Total purchased - total burned

## â“ Common Questions

**Q: Can I withdraw RC to cash?**  
A: No, RC tokens cannot be converted back to ETH. They exist only within the platform ecosystem.

**Q: Are RC tokens tradeable?**  
A: Not currently. RC is a utility token for gameplay, not a investment or tradeable asset.

**Q: What happens to RC when I lose a game?**  
A: Your staked RC is forfeited and added to the winner's pot.

**Q: Can I send RC to other players?**  
A: No direct transfers. RC can only move through game mechanics (stakes and winnings).

**Q: Is there a maximum RC I can hold?**  
A: No limit! Accumulate as much as you can earn.

**Q: Do RC tokens expire?**  
A: No, your balance never expires or decays.
