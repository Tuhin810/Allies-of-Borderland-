### How It Works
1. **Assignment**: At round start, you're given a random suit
2. **Hidden**: Only you know your suit (if you're a Citizen)
3. **Guessing**: During Jail phase, you must guess your suit
4. **Survival**: Correct guess = live, incorrect = eliminated
5. **Re-Assignment**: Survivors get new random suits next round

> **Important**: The Jack also receives a suit and must guess correctly to survive!

## ‚è±Ô∏è Game Phases

Each round consists of two distinct phases:

### Phase 1: Discussion (4 Minutes)

**What Happens**:
- All players can see each other via video
- Chat is enabled (voice and text)
- No game actions can be taken (just talk!)

**Your Goal**:
- Citizens: Identify the Jack through behavior
- Jack: Appear innocent, create doubt
- Everyone: Gather information about suits

**Strategies**:
- **Share Suit Info**: "I have Hearts, what about you?"
- **Accuse Others**: "Player 3 seems suspicious..."
- **Defend Yourself**: "I can't be the Jack, I survived last round!"
- **Form Alliances**: "Let's work together to find the Jack"
- **Read Body Language**: Watch for nervous ticks, lying cues

### Phase 2: Jail (60 Seconds)

**What Happens**:
- Video feed disabled (goes dark)
- Chat locked (no communication)
- UI shows 4 suit options: ‚ô• ‚ô¶ ‚ô£ ‚ô†
- Timer counts down from 60 seconds

**Your Action**:
1. Click on the suit you believe is yours
2. Selected suit highlights
3. Timer expires or you click "Confirm"
4. No going back after confirmation!

**Strategies**:
- **Trust Your Memory**: Recall what people said in Discussion
- **Probability**: If everyone claimed Hearts, maybe try something else
- **Intuition**: Sometimes your gut is right
- **Time Management**: Don't wait until the last second

### Phase 3: Resolution (Automatic)

**What Happens**:
- All players' results revealed simultaneously
- Correct guessers: ‚úÖ Survived (stay alive)
- Incorrect guessers: ‚ùå Eliminated (out of game)
- Game checks win conditions

**Possible Outcomes**:
- **Jack Eliminated** ‚Üí Citizens Win! (game ends)

- **Jack Alive + >2 Players** ‚Üí Next Round begins

## üéØ Strategy Tips

### As a Citizen

**Early Game**:
- Share your suit truthfully to build trust
- Observe who hesitates when asked about their suit
- Take note of aggressive accusers (could be Jack deflecting)

**Mid Game**:
- Track who survives consistently (unlikely to be guessing wrong)
- Watch for players who contradict themselves
- Form alliances but verify claims

**Late Game**:
- If it's down to 3-4 players, focus on process of elimination
- The Jack will try to cause chaos‚Äîstay calm and logical

### As the Jack

**Early Game**:
- Act like a Citizen: share "your suit" confidently
- Don't be too quiet (suspicious) or too loud (overcompensating)
- Accuse someone else to deflect attention

**Mid Game**:
- Sow discord: "I think Player 2 and 4 are working together..."
- If caught in a lie, play it off as misremembering

**Late Game**:
- If you're down to final few, lean into chaos
- Make everyone doubt each other
- Survive just one more round to win!

## ‚ùì FAQ

**Q: Can the Jack lie about their suit?**  
A: Yes! The Jack can claim any suit during Discussion.

**Q: What if I don't guess in time during Jail?**  
A: You're automatically eliminated (no guess = wrong guess).

**Q: Can I change my guess after clicking it?**  
A: Yes, until you click "Confirm" or time runs out.

**Q: Do eliminated players get anything back?**  
A: No, eliminated players lose their stake.

**Q: Can there be multiple Jacks?**  
A: No, there is always exactly one Jack per game.
