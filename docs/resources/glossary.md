# Glossary

Quick reference for common terms, abbreviations, and concepts used in Allies of Borderland.

---

## A

**AI (Artificial Intelligence)**  
Computer-controlled opponents in practice mode that simulate human behavior.

**Arena**  
The multiplayer lobby where players browse and join available game rooms.

---

## B

**Borderland**  
The metaphorical realm where the game takes place; symbolizes the space between trust and deception.

**Buy-In**  
The stake amount required to enter a game, set by the room host in multiplayer mode.

---

## C

**Citizen**  
The majority role in the game; players who must identify and eliminate the Jack.

**Clubs ♣**  
One of the four suits. Represents power and aggression.

---

## D

**Diamonds ♦**  
One of the four suits. Represents wealth and logic.

**Discussion Phase**  
The 4-minute period where players communicate via video/audio to deduce who the Jack is.

---

## E

**Elimination**  
When a player guesses their suit incorrectly and is removed from the game.

**ERC-20**  
Ethereum token standard that RC tokens follow for compatibility.

**ETH (Ethereum)**  
The cryptocurrency used to purchase RC tokens.

---

## G

**Guess**  
The action of selecting a suit during Jail phase to determine survival.

---

## H

**Hearts ♥**  
One of the four suits. Represents passion and emotion.

**Host**  
The player who creates a multiplayer room and controls game start.

---

## J

**Jack**  
The traitor role; one player whose goal is to deceive Citizens and survive.

**Jail Phase**  
The 60-second period where players privately select their suit guess with no communication allowed.

---

## L

**Lobby**  
The waiting room before a game starts where players can chat and prepare.

---

## M

**MetaMask**  
A cryptocurrency wallet browser extension used to connect to Allies of Borderland.

**Multiplayer**  
Competitive game mode where real human players compete against each other.

---

## P

**P2P (Peer-to-Peer)**  
The decentralized technology used for video/audio connections without central servers.

**Platform Fee**  
The 2% of each game pot taken by the platform to sustain operations.

**Pot**  
The total pool of staked RC tokens that winners will split.

**Practice Mode**  
Single-player mode where you play against AI opponents with no risk.

---

## R

**RC (Redemption Credits)**  
The native utility token used in Allies of Borderland for staking and rewards.

**Resolution**  
The automatic phase where suit guesses are revealed and eliminations occur.

**ROI (Return on Investment)**  
Percentage profit or loss from a game: (Winnings - Stake) ÷ Stake × 100%

**Round**  
One complete cycle of Discussion → Jail → Resolution phases.

---

## S

**Sepolia**  
The Ethereum test network currently used by Allies of Borderland (before mainnet launch).

**Single-Player**  
Practice mode where you play against AI bots.

**Spades ♠**  
One of the four suits. Represents death and mystery.

**Spectator**  
A player observing a game without participating or staking.

**Stake**  
The amount of RC tokens wagered in a game that winners will claim.

**Suit**  
One of four card types (Hearts, Diamonds, Clubs, Spades) randomly assigned each round.

**Survivor**  
A player who remains alive at game end and receives a share of the pot.

---

## T

**Token**  
Digital asset on blockchain; in this context, refers to RC tokens.

**Traitor**  
Another term for the Jack role.

---

## W

**Web3**  
The blockchain-powered internet ecosystem that Allies of Borderland is built on.

**WebRTC**  
Technology enabling peer-to-peer video/audio communication in browsers.

**Whitepaper**  
Technical document explaining the tokenomics and game design.

**Winner Pool**  
The 98% of the pot distributed to survivors (after 2% platform fee).
