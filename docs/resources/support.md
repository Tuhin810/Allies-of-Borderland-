## üìß Direct Support

### Email Support
**support@alliesofborderland.com**

**Response Time**: 24-48 hours

**When to Email**:
- Account issues (can't log in, lost tokens)
- Payment problems (transaction failed)
- Privacy concerns
- Legal questions
- Partnership inquiries

**Include in Email**:
- Your account username
- Detailed issue description
- Screenshots (if applicable)
- Browser/device information
- Transaction hash (for payment issues)

## üìã Community Guidelines

### Be Respectful
- No harassment, hate speech, or bullying
- Respect all players regardless of skill level
- Keep chat friendly and inclusive

### Fair Play
- No cheating, exploits, or multi-accounting
- No collusion in games
- Play to win, but be gracious in defeat

### Consequences
Violations may result in:
- Warning
- Temporary ban
- Permanent ban
- Token forfeit (serious cases)
