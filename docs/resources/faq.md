## üéÆ General Questions

### What is Allies of Borderland?
Allies of Borderland is a competitive social deduction game powered by Ethereum blockchain. Players stake RC tokens in matches where they must identify a hidden traitor (the Jack) while surviving suit-guessing rounds.

**Key Features**:
- Live video communication
- Real cryptocurrency stakes
- Skill-based competition
- 2% platform fee

### Is this gambling?
No. Allies of Borderland is a **skill-based game**, not gambling:
- Outcomes depend on psychological strategy, not chance
- Better players consistently win over time
- Similar to poker: randomness exists, but skill dominates
- No pure luck-based mechanics

### Is it free to play?
**Yes and no**:
- **Free to start**: New users receive 200 RC tokens (free)
- **Free practice mode**: Play against AI with no risk
- **Paid competitive**: Multiplayer requires staking RC tokens
- **No subscription**: One-time token purchases only

### What devices can I play on?
**Currently**:
- ‚úÖ Desktop (Windows, Mac, Linux) via web browser
- ‚úÖ Tablets (with camera/microphone)
- ‚ùå Mobile (limited support, UI not optimized)

**Coming Soon**:

### How do I get RC tokens?
1. **Free**: Receive 200 RC when creating an account
2. **Purchase**: Buy with ETH via MetaMask (0.0001 ETH per RC)

### Can I withdraw RC to real money?
**No.** RC tokens cannot be converted back to ETH or fiat currency. They exist only within the Allies of Borderland ecosystem.

**Why?**:
- Keeps the game outside gambling regulations
- Maintains focus on gameplay, not speculation
- Creates sustainable economy

### What happens to tokens when the game ends?
**If you win**:
- Receive your share of 98% of the pot
- Losers forfeit their stakes to you

**If you lose**:
- Your staked RC is lost
- Winners distribute it among themselves

**Platform**:
- Keeps 2% of every pot as fee

## üéØ Gameplay Questions

### How many players per game?
**Range**: 3-6 players

**Recommended**:
- **3-4 players**: Fast games, simpler dynamics
- **5 players**: Balanced (most common)
- **6 players**: Complex, chaotic, longer games

### How long does a game last?
**Average**: 15-30 minutes

**Breakdown**:
- Each round: 5 minutes (4 min discussion + 1 min jail)
- Typical game: 3-6 rounds
- Quick games: 10 minutes (fast eliminations)
- Long games: 45+ minutes (strategic play)

### Can I play without a camera?
**Single-player**: Yes, camera optional for AI practice mode

**Multiplayer**: Camera **strongly recommended**
- Social deduction relies on reading faces
- Players may kick you if you refuse camera
- Some rooms enforce camera requirement

### What if I disconnect mid-game?
**Your stake is lost**:
- Even if disconnection wasn't your fault
- Game continues without you
- Considered eliminated (no refund)

**To prevent**:
- Use stable internet connection
- Close unnecessary browser tabs
- Don't play during known network issues

### Can I pause the game?
**No.** Games run in real-time without pauses:
- Discussion phase: 4 minutes (countdown continues)
- Jail phase: 60 seconds (automatic)
- Bathroom breaks: Not available (plan ahead!)

## üí° Strategy Questions

### How do I know if I'm the Jack?
**You'll know immediately**:
- Role assignment screen shows "You are the Jack"
- Red highlight on your role indicator
- Special message in narrative

**If you're a Citizen**:
- Screen shows "You are a Citizen"
- Blue highlight on role
- You do NOT know who the Jack is

### How do I guess my suit?
**During Jail Phase**:
1. Four suit icons appear: ‚ô• ‚ô¶ ‚ô£ ‚ô†
2. Click on the suit you believe is yours
3. Confirm before time runs out (60 sec)
4. Wait for simultaneous reveal

**Tips**:
- Listen during Discussion phase for clues
- Track who claimed which suits
- Use probability (if everyone claims Hearts, try another)

### What's the best strategy as a Citizen?
**Early Game**:
- Share your suit truthfully to build trust
- Observe who hesitates or contradicts
- Take notes on claims

**Mid Game**:
- Form alliances with consistent players
- Call out suspicious behavior
- Verify claims with logic

**Late Game**:
- Use process of elimination
- Trust gut instincts on remaining players
- Stay calm under Jack's pressure

### What's the best strategy as the Jack?
**Key Tactics**:
- **Act natural**: Behave like a Citizen
- **Lie confidently**: Claim a suit and stick to it
- **Create chaos**: Accuse others to deflect
- **Stay consistent**: Don't contradict yourself
- **Blend in**: Don't be too quiet or too loud

**Advanced**:
- Frame someone else as suspicious
- Sow discord between Citizens
- Fake alliances then betray

### How do I install MetaMask?
1. Visit [metamask.io](https://metamask.io)
2. Click "Download"
3. Install browser extension
4. Create wallet (save seed phrase securely!)
5. Switch network to **Sepolia Testnet**

**See**: [Wallet Setup Guide ‚Üí](../getting-started/wallet-setup.md)

### What is Sepolia Testnet?
**Sepolia** is an Ethereum test network:
- Uses fake ETH (no real value)
- For testing blockchain apps before mainnet launch
- Free testnet ETH from faucets

**Current Status**: Allies of Borderland runs on Sepolia (testing phase)

**Future**: Will migrate to Ethereum Mainnet (real value)

## üí∞ Economics Questions

### How much should I stake?
**Beginner**: 10-25 RC (low risk, learn game)

**Intermediate**: 50-100 RC (moderate risk, competitive)

**Advanced**: 100-500 RC (high risk, high reward)

**Pro Tip**: Never stake more than you're willing to lose!

### What's the platform fee?
**2% of every game pot**

**Example**:
- 5 players stake 100 RC each
- Total pot: 500 RC
- Platform fee: 10 RC (2%)
- Winner pool: 490 RC

**Why the fee?**
- Sustains platform development
- Covers server and infrastructure costs
- Funds future features and tournaments

### Can I profit from this game?
**Yes, if you're skilled**:
- Skilled players achieve >51% win rate
- Positive expected value over time
- Similar to poker: skill matters more than luck

**No guarantees**:
- Beginners often lose while learning
- Even experts lose individual games
- Variance exists (short-term luck)

### What's ROI (Return on Investment)?
**ROI** = (Winnings - Stake) √∑ Stake √ó 100%

**Examples**:
- Stake 100 RC, win 200 RC ‚Üí ROI = +100%
- Stake 100 RC, lose ‚Üí ROI = -100%
- Win as sole survivor (4-player game, 400 RC pot):
  - Receive 392 RC (after 2% fee)
  - ROI = (392 - 100) / 100 = +292%

## üö® Problem Solving

### "Insufficient balance" error
**You need**:
- Stake amount + room creation fee (if hosting)
- Example: 100 RC buy-in + 10 RC host fee = 110 RC required

**Solutions**:
- Purchase more RC tokens
- Lower the stake amount
- Join existing rooms instead of hosting

### Game is laggy / freezing
**Performance Tips**:
1. Close other browser tabs
2. Close bandwidth-heavy apps (downloads, streaming)
3. Lower video quality in settings
4. Use wired ethernet (not WiFi)
5. Restart browser

### Can't connect to MetaMask
**Troubleshooting**:
1. Ensure MetaMask extension is installed
2. Unlock MetaMask (enter password)
3. Switch to **Sepolia Testnet** in MetaMask
4. Refresh the page
5. Clear browser cache

### Audio not working
1. Grant microphone permission
2. Check browser mic settings
3. Test mic in browser settings
4. Close competing apps (Discord, etc.)
5. Try different browser

## üìú Legal & Safety

### Is my data safe?
**Yes**:
- Video/audio is P2P (not stored on servers)
- Minimal personal data collection
- Ethereum transactions are public (blockchain nature)
- See [Privacy Policy](../legal/privacy-policy.md)

### Is this legal in my country?
**Generally yes**, but check local laws:
- Skill-based gaming is legal in most jurisdictions
- No fiat currency withdrawal (not traditional gambling)
- Players responsible for compliance with local regulations

**Restricted Regions**: None currently, but subject to change

### Can I get scammed?
**Platform is safe**:
- Open-source smart contracts (auditable)
- No direct player-to-player transfers
- All transactions on-chain (transparent)

**Beware of**:
- Fake websites (only use official domain)
- Phishing MetaMask popups
- "Too good to be true" RC offers
