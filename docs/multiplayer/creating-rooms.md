# Creating Rooms

Host your own custom game by creating a multiplayer room. This guide covers room creation, configuration options, and host responsibilities.

---

## üéÆ Overview

As a **room host**, you control:
- **Buy-in amount**: How much players must stake
- **Discussion time**: Length of debate phases
- **Player capacity**: Maximum number of participants
- **Game start**: When the match begins

**Cost to Create Room**: **10 RC** (platform fee)

---

## üìù Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Navigate to Arena
1. From the main menu, click **"Arena"**
2. You'll see the multiplayer lobby with existing rooms
3. Click **"Create Room"** button (top-right)

### Step 2: Configure Room Settings

You'll see the **Room Creation Modal** with the following options:

#### Buy-In Amount
- **Range**: 1 RC - 1000 RC
- **Default**: 50 RC
- **Description**: The stake amount ALL players (including you) must pay

**Example**: If you set 100 RC buy-in:
- 5 players join
- Total pot = 500 RC
- Platform fee (2%) = 10 RC
- Winner pool = 490 RC

#### Discussion Time
- **Range**: 2-10 minutes
- **Default**: 4 minutes
- **Description**: How long each Discussion phase lasts

**Recommendations**:
- **2-3 min**: Fast-paced, high-pressure games
- **4 min**: Balanced (recommended)
- **6-10 min**: Deep strategy, longer debates

#### Maximum Players
- **Range**: 3-6 players
- **Default**: 5 players
- **Description**: Room capacity (including host)

**Player Count Impact**:
- **3 players**: Quick, intense, harder for Jack
- **4-5 players**: Balanced gameplay
- **6 players**: Complex social dynamics, more chaos

### Step 3: Confirm Creation
1. Review your settings
2. Ensure you have sufficient balance:
   - Room creation fee: **10 RC**
   - Buy-in amount: **Your chosen stake**
   - Total needed: **10 + stake amount**
3. Click **"Create Room"**
4. MetaMask may prompt for confirmation (if using wallet)
5. ‚úÖ Room created! You're now in the **Lobby**

## üè† Room Lobby (Host View)

Once your room is created, you'll see:

### Left Panel: Room Info
- **Room ID**: Unique identifier (shareable link)
- **Host**: Your username (marked as Host)
- **Status**: "Waiting for Players"
- **Settings**: Buy-in, discussion time, capacity

### Center: Player List
- Your video feed (if camera enabled)
- Empty slots for incoming players
- Player count: "2/5" (example)

### Right Panel: Chat
- System messages: "Player X joined"
- Pre-game chat with players

### Bottom: Host Controls
- **Copy Room Link**: Share with friends
- **Start Game**: Begin when ready (requires 3+ players)
- **Close Room**: Cancel and refund fee

---

## üöÄ Starting the Game

### Requirements
- **Minimum Players**: 3 (including you)
- **All Players Ready**: Everyone has camera/audio enabled
- **Host Paid**: You've been charged the buy-in amount

### How to Start
1. Wait for desired number of players to join
2. Click **"Start Game"** button
3. Confirm in pop-up: "Are you sure?"
4. Game countdown begins (5 seconds)
5. üéÆ Game starts!

**Note**: Once started, no more players can join. Late arrivals become spectators (if enabled).

## üîó Sharing Your Room

### Option 1: Direct Link
1. Click **"Copy Room Link"** in lobby
2. Share the URL with friends via:
   - Discord
   - Twitter
   - Email
   - Messaging apps
3. Anyone with the link can join (if lobby is open)

**Example Link**:
```
https://allies-of-borderland.com/lobby?room=abc123xyz
```

### Option 2: Room Code
Alternatively, share just the **Room ID** (visible in lobby):
```
Room ID: abc123xyz
```

Players can manually enter this code in the "Join Room" field.

## ‚ùì Common Questions

**Q: Can I change settings after creating the room?**  
A: No, settings are locked once room is created. Close and recreate if needed.

**Q: What if no one joins my room?**  
A: You can close the room and get your 10 RC fee refunded (stake is not deducted until game starts).

**Q: Can I play as a spectator in my own room?**  
A: No, the host is always a player (stakes required).

**Q: What happens if I disconnect as host?**  
A: The room closes and players are refunded. Try to maintain stable internet!

**Q: Can I kick a player from my room?**  
A: Yes, in the lobby (before game starts). Right-click their video ‚Üí "Kick".

## üéØ Best Practices

### For Beginners
- Start with **50 RC buy-in** (moderate stakes)
- Use **4-minute discussion** (standard)
- Aim for **4-5 players** (balanced)

### For Competitive Players
- **100-500 RC buy-in** (high stakes)
- **6-8 minute discussion** (deep strategy)
- **Require camera** (enforce via chat)

### For Friend Groups
- **Low stakes (10-25 RC)** for casual fun
- **Longer discussion (8-10 min)** for socializing
- **Share room link privately** via Discord/group chat

## üö® Troubleshooting

### "Insufficient balance to create room"
- You need **10 RC fee + your stake amount**
- Example: 100 RC buy-in requires 110 RC total
- Purchase more tokens or lower the buy-in

### Players can't join my room
- Check that room isn't full (capacity reached)
- Ensure players meet buy-in requirement
- Verify they have camera/audio access

### Room disappeared after creation
- Browser crash or connection loss
- Contact support with Room ID for investigation
